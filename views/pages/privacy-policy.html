Cookies

A cookie, is usually a small piece of data sent from a website and stored in user’s local hard disk when you browse a website. Cookies can be retrieved by website to notify the website of the user’s previous activity, which can helps the website customize service for you. TP-LINK and our partners may sometimes record, track and use your cookies only for audit, data analysis and research, for example, to count Page View, to assess which content is more popular and to be improved. We may also use cookies to help complete online order or transaction. You can configure your web browser to alert or accept or refuse when websites try to create cookies to your computer. Also, you can delete cookies on your computer. But please attention that some web pages may not work correctly if you disable cookies.
